Effective Date: 17-SEP-2024

MindReader App ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle user data and privacy when using our app. We believe in providing a safe and transparent experience for all users, including children. Please read the following carefully to understand our practices regarding your privacy.

1. Information Collection

The MindReader App does not collect any personal information from users. We do not gather, store, or share any of your personal data, including:

  • Name
  • Email address

2. Permissions

Our app does not request any permissions from your device. It does not access any of your phone's features like the camera, microphone, contacts, or location services.

3. Children's Privacy

We are fully compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). Our app is designed to be suitable for users of all ages, including children. Since we do not collect any personal information, there is no risk of children's personal data being stored or shared.

4. Third-Party Services

The MindReader App does not use any third-party services such as analytics tools, advertisements, or social media plugins that could collect or share your data.

5. Security

Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, there are no specific security measures needed to protect user information. However, we always strive to ensure the app is safe and secure for all users.
