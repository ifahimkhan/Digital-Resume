Chess Timer Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 15, 2023
Summary: This app does not collect, store, or share any personal user data. All timer information exists only on your device.

1. Information We Collect

This app does not collect, store, or share any personal user data, including:

  • No names, emails, or contact information
  • No game history or move data
  • No device identifiers (IMEI, MAC address)
  • No location data

2. Permissions

The app requests:

  • VIBRATE: For timeout haptic feedback (optional)
  • INTERNET (optional): Only for crash reporting via Google Play Services

3. Third-Party Services

  • Google Play Services: Anonymous crash data collection (can be disabled in device settings)
  • No ads or analytics trackers

4. Data Security

All timer data exists only on your device and is erased when:

  • You close the app
  • You press "Reset"
  • You uninstall the app
